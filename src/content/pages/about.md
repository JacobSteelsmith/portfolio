# About Me

## Professional Summary

I'm a cloud platform engineer and technical leader with extensive experience designing, building, and operating production infrastructure on AWS. I focus on helping organizations modernize their platforms through serverless architectures, infrastructure-as-code, and DevOps best practices.

My work spans the full lifecycle — from architecture and design through implementation, deployment, and operational excellence. I believe in building systems that are observable, resilient, and cost-effective.

## Expertise Areas

- **Cloud Platform Engineering** — Designing and operating production AWS environments at scale
- **AWS Modernization** — Migrating legacy workloads to cloud-native architectures
- **Serverless & Distributed Systems** — Building event-driven applications with Lambda, Step Functions, EventBridge, and SQS
- **Infrastructure as Code** — CloudFormation, Terraform, and CDK for reproducible, version-controlled infrastructure
- **CI/CD & DevOps** — GitHub Actions, CodePipeline, automated testing, and deployment pipelines
- **Security & Compliance** — IAM least-privilege, encryption at rest and in transit, OIDC federation
- **Observability** — CloudWatch, X-Ray, structured logging, and operational dashboards
- **Technical Leadership** — Mentoring engineers, establishing standards, and driving architectural decisions

## Career Narrative

My career in technology started in the mid-2000s with web development — ColdFusion, JavaScript, and the early days of AJAX. Over the years, I've evolved alongside the industry, moving from traditional server-based architectures to cloud-native platforms.

Today, I specialize in AWS platform engineering, where I help teams build the foundations they need to ship reliably and scale confidently. Whether it's designing a multi-account strategy, implementing zero-trust networking, or building a deployment pipeline that gives developers fast feedback, I'm focused on the infrastructure that makes great software possible.

I'm passionate about the intersection of engineering excellence and practical delivery — building things the right way without losing sight of business outcomes.
