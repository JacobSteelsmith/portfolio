Perhaps the number one reason Firefox is so popular is due to the Adblock extension. Adblock filters out advertisements from websites. To download Adblock, go to [the Adblock page located in the extensions portion of the Firefox website](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/10/). Once you install Adblock, you'll also want to install the [Adblock filterset.G Updater](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1136/) which is an extension that updates the advertisements to block automatically!

Nothing else is required to configure this extension, although you can play with the options by going to **tools -> Adblock**. Next I will highlight themes and my favorite one, which is also the most popular.
