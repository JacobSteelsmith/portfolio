---
title: "Open Source Alternatives: Quanta Plus"
date: 2007-04-28
---

A must have tool for web developers is [Quanta Plus](http://quanta.kdewebdev.org/ "Quanta Plus"), a multi-purpose development tool. It supports code highlighting for many languages and applications, FTP, projects, plugins and much more. To be honest, I have barely scratched the surface of it's capability. Half the time, it is easier to open a document in [Kate](http://kate-editor.org/ "Kate") and edit it. Kate is the notepad of Linux, but on steroids.
