title: "Open Source Alternatives: Kopete"
date: 2007-04-27
---

Kopete is a multi-network, multi-account instant messenger client for use with Linux systems. I run [Kubuntu Fiesty Fawn](http://www.kubuntu.org/ "Kubuntu Fiesty") and use this exclusively. I have two msn accounts, a yahoo account and an AIM account. Kopete supports 11 IM clients out of the box.
