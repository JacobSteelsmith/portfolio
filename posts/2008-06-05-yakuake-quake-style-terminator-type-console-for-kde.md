title: "Yakuake quake style, terminator type console for kde"
date: 2008-06-05
---

[Yakuake](http://www.kde-apps.org/content/show.php?content=29153) is a quake style, terminator type terminal emulator, or console for KDE.
With the rising popularity of [terminator](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/terminator-multiple-gnome-terminals-in-one-window.html) for gnome, which is very cool, I love that KDE has an equivalent.
![yakuake screen shot](/files/drop-down-console.png)
Not only does the console drop down from the top of your screen on a key press (the quake style) and animate back up on the same key press, but it also has the ability to split into multiple terminals in the same window, just like terminator.
